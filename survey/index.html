    INFO: Data science experience

    A data scientist is defined but their capability to combine a multitude of skills, not simply be an expert in one particular area. Drew Conway's Data Science venn diagram (below) is currently the most widely adopted visualisation of what a data scientist is.

    Studies[][] have revealed a skills gap in data scientists globally.
